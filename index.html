about me

My name is Will, but many people call me Willy, and I'm mainly a content creator and osu! player. osu! is a rhythm game where you tap and aim circles to the beat of the music. I don't consider myself good at it because I never can perform at the level of my expectations. Some of the projects I've worked on are listed below.
